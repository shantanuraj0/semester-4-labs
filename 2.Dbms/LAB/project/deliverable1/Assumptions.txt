---->When a Seller comes to the office to list a property for sale or rent, he/she will interact with an
agent who is assigned by the office. That agent is responsible for managing the property and
updating theseller table by the details of the owner.

----->When a buyer visits the office to buy/rent a property, the office assigns an agent to the client
based on his/her requirements(assuming requirements and assigning agent to be VERBAL). On
a successful transaction of property the agent is responsible for updating property status and
buyers entity with appropriate details.
